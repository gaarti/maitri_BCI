Vision & Mission

Vision

At BCI Skills, we work with you, assisting you to recognise and value your full potential. Applauding your success is what drives us – we’re committed to the long-term vision of your professional development. People who deliver their best become role-models for others within the work environment – raising the bar and generating even more success.

Mission

When you BELIEVE in your own potential, you BROADEN your professional options and BENEFIT from the strategically planned professional success that follows. When it comes to BEING THERE for you, the BCI Skills team provides all the career support you need, ensuring you enjoy the experience of career development and achieve your goals.

Director

Manoj is an expert in strategic management and business development through India and around the world. He has enjoyed a highly successful career in a number of large Indian businesses including Reliance, Koshika Telecom Ltd and Bharti Telematics as well as currently delivering employment outcomes as the Deputy Director (Placement & Strategic Affairs) for KIIT School of Management. Recent successes have improved the standing of the School within the corporate sector whilst still delivering classes of more than 150 students.

As a Director of Bravo India, Manoj brings his relationships and knowledge of corporate requirements for the benefit of all students. With a focus on developing quality living within India, Manoj is delivering on the promise of finding the best balance of classroom learning and practical exposure to turn students into the best professionals and leaders they can be.
